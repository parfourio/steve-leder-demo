About Rabbi Steve Leder

Guiding people through
life's hardest questions.

Steve Leder is the Senior Rabbi of Wilshire Boulevard Temple in Los Angeles — one of America's most historic and storied congregations. After graduating cum laude from Northwestern University and studying at Trinity College, Oxford, he received his master's and rabbinical ordination from Hebrew Union College.

He is the author of five books, including the bestsellers The Beauty of What Remains and More Beautiful Than Before, and his latest, For You When I Am Gone. His writing has helped hundreds of thousands of readers find meaning in loss, suffering, and the everyday moments we too easily overlook.

Newsweek Magazine has twice named him one of the ten most influential rabbis in America. Most importantly to Steve, he is Betsy's husband, and Aaron and Hannah's dad — and a Jew who likes to fish.
